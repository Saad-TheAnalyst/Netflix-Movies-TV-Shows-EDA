# Netflix Movies & TV Shows EDA

## Overview
An in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis of Netflix Movies 
and TV Shows dataset using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib 
and Power BI to uncover content trends, director patterns, 
genre popularity and platform growth from 2008 to 2021.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Power BI
- JupyterLab

## Questions Answered
- How many Movies vs TV Shows are on Netflix?
- Which country produces the most Netflix content?
- Which year had the most content added?
- Which month does Netflix add the most content?
- Who are the most prolific Netflix directors?
- What are the most common ratings?
- What are the most popular genres?
- How long are Netflix movies on average?
- How old is content when added to Netflix?
- What are the most common words in Netflix descriptions?

## Key Findings

### Content Distribution
- Netflix has 6,131 Movies and 2,666 TV Shows 
  meaning Movies make up 69.7% of all content.
- The United States produces the most Netflix 
  content with 2,812 titles followed by India 
  with 972 titles.

### Content Addition Trends
- 2019 was the peak year for content added 
  with 2,016 titles added in that year alone.
- July and December are the most popular months 
  for Netflix to add new content with 827 and 
  813 titles respectively.
- January sees the least content added showing 
  Netflix strategically times releases.

### Directors
- Rajiv Chilaka is the most prolific Netflix 
  director with 19 titles.
- Martin Scorsese and Steven Spielberg both 
  feature in the top 10 most prolific directors 
  with 12 and 11 titles respectively.

### Movie Duration
- The average Netflix movie is 100 minutes long.
- The longest movie on Netflix is 312 minutes.
- The shortest movie is just 3 minutes long.

### TV Shows
- The average Netflix TV show has 1.8 seasons.
- The longest running Netflix TV show has 17 seasons.

### Content Age
- On average Netflix adds content that is 4.7 years 
  old showing they mix new and older content.
- The oldest content added was 93 years old when 
  it appeared on Netflix.

### Ratings
- TV-MA is the most common rating with 3,205 titles 
  showing Netflix focuses heavily on mature content.
- TV-14 comes second with 2,157 titles.

### Genres
- International Movies is the most popular genre 
  with 2,752 titles followed by Dramas with 2,427.

### Most Common Description Words
- The most common words in Netflix descriptions 
  are young, family, world and love showing Netflix 
  content focuses heavily on family and relationship themes.

### Platform Growth
- Netflix grew from just 2 titles in 2008 to over 
  2,016 titles in 2019 — a 100,700% increase showing 
  massive platform expansion over the years.
- Content addition slowed in 2020 and 2021 likely 
  due to COVID-19 production delays.
