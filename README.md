# Netflix Movies & TV Shows EDA

## Overview
Exploratory Data Analysis of Netflix Movies and TV Shows 
dataset using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib and Power BI 
to uncover content trends, popular genres, and 
growth patterns on the platform.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Power BI
- JupyterLab

## Questions Answered
- How many Movies vs TV Shows are on Netflix?
- Which country produces the most Netflix content?
- Which year had the most content added?
- What are the most common ratings?
- What are the most popular genres?
- How has Netflix content grown over the years?

## Key Findings
- Netflix has 6,131 Movies and 2,666 TV Shows 
  meaning Movies make up 69.7% of all content.

- The United States produces the most Netflix 
  content with 2,812 titles followed by 
  India with 972 titles.

- 2019 was the peak year for content added 
  with 2,016 titles added in that year alone.

- TV-MA is the most common rating with 3,205 
  titles showing Netflix focuses heavily on 
  mature audience content.

- International Movies is the most popular 
  genre with 2,752 titles followed by 
  Dramas with 2,427 titles.

- Netflix content grew rapidly from just 2 
  titles in 2008 to over 2,016 titles in 2019 
  showing massive platform expansion over the years.

Kaggle - Netflix Movies and TV Shows Dataset
